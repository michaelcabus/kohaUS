koha-US for Better Libraries

koha-US is about more than just Koha software; it's about empowering libraries. Whether it's through educational workshops that help librarians server their patrons better, or through the funding of new software features that help staff with their workflows or help patrons connect to library content, koha-US is about using technology to improve librares.

Become a koha-US member today, or donate, to ensure koha-US continues to be an innovative force for libraries and Koha
