Join Our Listserv

Are you new to using Koha? Are you looking for a community of Koha enthusiasts? Join the Koha U.S. listserv!

With members throughout the United States, you will be able to connect with a wellspring of collective Koha wisdom. Get updates on, and discuss new features, and learn about the innovative ways other libraries are using Koha. Join us!

Find a Koha Mentor

A Koha mentor is a member of Koha U.S. who can be a point of contact as you setup Koha for your library.

All Koha U.S. mentors are working librarians, working in a variety of library settings (from academic, to special, to public). Connect with a mentor today to make your transition to Koha a success!
