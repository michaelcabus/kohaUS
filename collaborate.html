Join Our Listserv

Are you new to using Koha? Are you looking for a community of Koha enthusiasts? Join the koha-US listserv!

With members throughout the United States, you will be able to connect with a wellspring of collective Koha wisdom. Get updates on, and discuss new features, and learn about the innovative ways other libraries are using Koha. Join us!

Find a Koha Mentor

A Koha mentor is a member of koha-US who can be a point of contact as you setup Koha for your library.

A benefit of being a due paying member of koha-US is the koha-US Koha mentorship program. All koha-US mentors are working librarians, working in a variety of library settings (from academic, to special, to public).

If you are a current member and would like to participate in the mentorship program, contact Christopher Brannon atFind a Mentor Now!

Join koha-US

Members of koha-US join a group of active, knowledgeable Koha users.

Through collaboration, koha-US members develop new skills, make new friends, and contribute to Koha's future.
